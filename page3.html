Q: Yitong, where do all your internet handles come from?

A: NitroNuke comes from a dumb joke I made as part of a project when I was maybe 7 years old which happened to stick. Natani was the name of the first character I played in Dungeons and Dragons, which I picked because the name sounded androgynous so it could easily be reused. Tyogin is the handle I picked up most recently to make myself look presentable to the internet. It's just an anagram of my first name.

Q: What's with your crazy bleached hair?

A: In high school I was struggling to manage time and had to drop out of extracurriculars to study and work, but I still wanted something unique to define myself. I picked hair. I still think it was a good call. Got it done at David's Hair Design, check it out if you're in London.

Q: Were your parents cool with it?

A: I told them in advance that I was going to do it, and they thought I was joking. They were not happy for a little bit when they realized I followed through. After a while they got used to it though.

Q: Was journalism your first choice?

A: Third, actually. I wanted to pursue music through most of high school. By the end it turned out I had neither the talent or skill to follow through on it as a career. After that I considered an English degree, but realized analyzing every piece of literature wasn't for me. Journalism ended up being a nice blend of creativity and writing for me, where I could also make use of the auditory skills I picked up through music.

Q: What do you hope to do after your degree?

A: I'd really just love to find a nice peaceful desk job somewhere. If I do somehow find myself in the field of jounalism even remotely though, I'd like to work with audio. Radio or podcasts would both be fields that interest me.

A: I took a first aid and AED course once
